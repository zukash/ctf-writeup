Sunny Side Up Eggs Recipe by ChatGPT

Ingredients:
- 2 large eggs
- 1 tablespoon butter or oil
- Salt, to taste
- Pepper, to taste

Instructions:
1. Heat a non-stick skillet over medium heat.
2. Add butter or oil to the skillet and let it melt.
3. Crack the eggs into the skillet, being careful not to break the yolks.
4. Cook the eggs until the whites are set but the yolks are still runny, about 2-3 minutes.
5. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
6. Serve immediately.

Enjoy your sunny side up eggs!
